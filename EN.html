FAQ

Answers to your most common questions.

Does it really understand Cantonese?

Yes. Oneder Chat is specifically tuned for Hong Kong. It understands colloquial Cantonese (slang), English, and Mandarin, switching between them automatically.

Do I need to install an App?

No. Everything is cloud-based. We connect via the WhatsApp Business API on the backend. You can continue using your existing WhatsApp account/number.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted with enterprise-grade AES-256. Servers are located locally in Hong Kong, fully compliant with GDPR and HK privacy laws. We do not share data with third parties.

Can I customize the AI tone?

Yes. We train the AI to match your brand's voice (e.g., professional, friendly, casual) and use your custom FAQ templates.
